**) Flash Messages
jab bhi app kisi ejs page ko dekhenege waha par aapko kisi parkar ka koi highlighted information dena hai, then it is called Flash Messages.
This is more like a good-looking alerts, warnings and descriptions.

In this, user give something on the frontend (like email and password), then that details goes to the backend server for checking.
if the login credentials are wrong, then a warning flash messgae was send from the server to the frontend as Flash Message.

Flash Messages ka means server k kisi route mein koi data banana and us data ko doosre route me use kar pana.
